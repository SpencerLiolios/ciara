Dr. Sarah Johnson

Certified Gallup Strengths Coach & Leadership Development Expert

With over a decade of experience in strengths-based coaching and organizational development, I've dedicated my career to helping individuals and teams unlock their full potential through the transformative power of CliftonStrengths.

My journey began in corporate leadership, where I witnessed firsthand how understanding and leveraging natural talents could dramatically improve both individual satisfaction and team performance. This revelation led me to pursue specialized training in strengths coaching and positive psychology.

Today, I work with professionals across industries, couples seeking deeper connection, and teams aiming for peak collaboration. My approach combines scientific rigor with genuine empathy, creating a safe space for meaningful discovery and growth.
